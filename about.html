About Me

My name is Jacob Rogers, I'm 21, and I'm currently a student at KSU. I am a Cybersecurity major with a minor in IT. I have a passion for Cybersecurity and have been enrolled here since August of 2020. I have not had any experience in the Cybersecurity field yet, but I love all things computers.

While I was in high school, I had a surveying job. This helped me realize that blue-collar jobs were not for me. In 2021 I had a job for a tech company where I worked on refurbishing various electronics. This included Apple and Windows desktops, iPads, etc. In the summer of 2023, I had another warehouse job at an aerospace fabrication company. I enjoyed this job as I got to learn about airplane parts and even help make some of them.

Outside of academics, I enjoy gaming and golfing. I used to be into mountain biking for many years, but I eventually grew out of that. I'm excited to continue my journey in Cybersecurity and explore new opportunities.
